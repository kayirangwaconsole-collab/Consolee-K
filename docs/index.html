Challenge & Opportunity

Understanding the Crisis

Over 258 million children remain out of school globally, with conflict-affected regions facing disproportionate barriers to education access. Infrastructure damage, displacement, and resource constraints threaten educational continuity and development opportunities for vulnerable populations.

📊

Data-Driven Solutions

Leverage comprehensive geospatial data to identify educational infrastructure gaps and prioritize interventions.

🎯

Targeted Impact

Focus resources on the 15 most vulnerable countries across three critical regions with the highest need.

🚀

Scalable Framework

Adaptable model that can be expanded to additional regions and infrastructure types.

🤝

Collaborative Approach

Integration with local stakeholders, NGOs, and government partners for sustainable implementation.
